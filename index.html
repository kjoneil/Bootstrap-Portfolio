About Me

Growing up in a small town in Iowa, I lived in the country on a farm. Surrounded by corn and soy bean fields my closest neighbor was a mile away. After graduating from the University of Iowa it was an easy decision to move into a big city.

For the past few years I have called Chicago home, where my closet neighbor in now across the hall. Striving to become a successful business professional through newtworking and development of a unique skill-set.
